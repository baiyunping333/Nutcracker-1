template<typename DimensionRange >
vector<unsigned int> Nutcracker::flatIndexToTensorIndex ( DimensionRange const &  dimensions,
unsigned long long  flat_index 
)

Converts an integral index of the flat representation of a tensor into the corresponding multi-index of the multi-dimensional representation of a tensor.

Note:
The conversion assumes that the tensor is stored in row-major order --- that is, an increment in the first entry of the multi index causes the greatest increase in the flat index and an increment in the last entry of the multi-index causes the least increase in the flast index.
Template Parameters:
DimensionRangethe type of the list of the dimensions
Parameters:
dimensionsthe dimension of the tensor
flat_indexthe index into the flattened tensor
Returns:
the multi-dimensional index referring to the same element in the original multi-dimensional tensor as flat_index had referred to in the flattened one-dimensional representation of that tensor.
