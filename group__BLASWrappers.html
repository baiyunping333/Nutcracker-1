Compute the 2-norm of a vector.

"C" double dznrm2_(uint32_t const* n, complex<double>* const x, uint32_t const* incx);

This function is just a convenience wrapper around the BLAS dznrm2 function.

Parameters:
nthe size of the vector
xa pointer to the vector data
incxthe increment between vector entries (usually 1)
Returns:
the 2-norm of the vector --- that is, the sum of the absolute-square of the entries
